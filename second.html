What types of devices do you repair?

▼

What brands and models do you work on?

▼

We work on a wide range of brands and models, including the latest iPhones, Android smartphones, and various desktop and laptop computers.

How long does a typical repair take?

▼

The time required for a repair can vary depending on the issue and the availability of parts, but we aim to complete most repairs within 1-3 business days.
